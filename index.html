ABOUT

What is PYM?

VISION

Renewed youth within the Basic Ecclesial Community of the Diocese of Tagbilaran living in the unity and love, with strong faith and trust in God as Disciples of Christ who are joyful and alive towards the kingdom of God.

MISSION

(1)To participate and share with those who need our time, talent,and treasure both in church activities and of society, living our priestly, kingly, and prophetic lives. (2) To respect and take care of our mother earth/creation especially the dignity of the person. (3) To cultivate and nourish worthwhile Philippine culture.

GOALS

To give and bring into reality the Basic Ecclesial Community and strengthen it through adequate implementation of a comprehensive program that leads us to achieve a holistic renewal as young evangelizers.
